Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the Leadstar Academy is the best school?

Leadstar Academy has an excellent reputation with experienced teachers, a wide range of courses, and modern facilities. There is a strong emphasis on student achievement, quality education, and providing a safe and supportive learning environment. You can trust that you or your child will receive the best possible education at Leadstar Academy.

What kind of learning environment is available?

Our school offers a collaborative learning environment that encourages students to work together and develop their skills. We also have access to a variety of digital and digital-enabled resources to support learning.

What types of extracurricular activities are offered?

We have a wide range of extracurricular activities available such as mini media, art, drama, sports, and basic Computer trial.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

The student-teacher ratio at our school is 50:1, meaning there are 18 students for every teacher in the classroom.

How do you support students with special needs?

We provide individualized supports for students with special needs, including specialized instruction and accommodations, as needed.

What is the school's approach to discipline?

Leadstar Academy has a holistic approach to discipline that focuses on positive reinforcement and building relationships between teachers, students, and parents. We believe in teaching our students appropriate behavior through setting clear expectations and consistent consequences. We also strive to create a safe and respectful learning environment for all students and ensure that all students are treated fairly and with respect. Our discipline system is designed to help students develop self-discipline and respect for themselves, for others, and for their environment.

What kind of support is offered to students and their families?

Leadstar Academy offers a variety of support services to students and their families. We have a team of dedicated teachers, counselors, and administrators who are committed to helping our students succeed. Our counseling staff provides individual and group counseling to help students manage their emotions, develop social skills, and build self-esteem. We also offer a range of family support services, such as parent-teacher conferences, workshops, and mentoring programs. Additionally, our comprehensive academic services include tutoring, extended learning opportunities, and guidance for college and career pathways. Finally, we have a team of dedicated volunteers who provide extra support to our students and families.

What are the top three things you can do to best prepare student for college success?

Leadstar Academy can best prepare students for college success by focusing on the following three key areas: 1.Academic Rigor: Encouraging students to challenge themselves through honors and advanced classes, as well as dedicated study time and test prep. 2.Career Exploration: Helping students to explore their interests and career paths through internships, job shadowing, and college visits. 3.Mentorship: Connecting students with mentors who can help guide them through the college application process and provide advice on academic and career goals.

How does the leadstar academy school measure success for students?

Leadstar Academy measures success for students by tracking their academic performance, attendance, and assessments. Academic performance is evaluated by tracking grades in each subject, as well as individual test scores. Attendance is monitored to ensure that students are attending classes regularly and meeting school requirements. Assessments are also used to evaluate student progress and identify areas where improvement is needed. All of these indicators are used to measure a student's success and help the school community provide the best possible education for its students.

What resources and support does Leadstar Academy provide to help high schoolers succeed?

Leadstar Academy provides a variety of resources and support to help high schoolers succeed. Our tutoring services provide personalized instruction in a variety of subjects to help students build a strong foundation for academic success. We also offer test prep and college counseling services to help navigate the college admissions process. Additionally, we provide workshops and seminars to help students develop important skills such as organization, time management, and self-advocacy. Our online learning platform provides access to a wide range of resources, such as study guides, practice tests, and sample college essays. Our experienced staff is always available to answer questions and provide individualized assistance to ensure that students have the support they need to reach their goals.
